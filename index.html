About us

Here at Bella's, we strive for great eats made with the highest quality ingredients. Our restaurant has a proud history of great service and a passion for Italian cuisine. As soon as you enter, you are welcomed by the aroma of our freshly made thin-crust pizzas, homemade sandwiches, savory pasta dishes, and much more. Located right on Main Street in the middle of Chester, Bella's is a must-try for authentic Italian food.
